About

My name is Alan and I'm the founder of Landoro Holdings LLC. After years in the tech industry, I felt a calling for something more rooted, tangible, and ever lasting. That's when I discovered the power and promise of land. Traveling across the vast American West, from red desert mesas to quiet pine valleys, I was amazed by the sense of freedom land represents. Every parcel tells a story of independence, potential, and legacy. Today, through Landoro Holdings, I help others find their own foothold in this great landscape. Whether you're building a future, preserving wealth, or just looking for peace under open skies, I'm here to help you claim your piece of the American dream.
